Kakegurui

Plot

At the prestigious Hyakkaou Private Academy only one thing matters. Gambling.

In this anime we follow Jabami Yumeko who is a transfer student. A classmate worries for her sake when she is invited to a gamble on her first day with a known bully of their class.

However, his worry is short-lived. Yumeko proves herself to be a worthy student of this school by seeing through the cheating of her classmate. In the following gambles intiated by people higher and higher in power, she continues showing why this school should worry of her presence.
