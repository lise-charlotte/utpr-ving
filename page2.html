Music

Music is subjective.

I think a lot of people can agree when I say music plays an important part of our lives. Even for folks who do not listen much, certain events like weddings tend to have music and along with the memory, the music sticks. Maybe one day you are watching a movie and suddenly the same song comes on. It sparks memories of the wedding. This goes for our entire lives.

A few songs that spark emotions and memories for me are;

  • Motionless In White - Catharsis
  • Red - Take It All Away
  • Lady Gaga - Monster, Bloody Mary, Heavy Metal Lover, Electric Chapel, Bad Romance, Paparazzi, Alejandro, Dance In The Dark, So Happy I Could Die
